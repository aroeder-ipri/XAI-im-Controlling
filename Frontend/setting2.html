Introduction to the Task

You will be asked for the sales target which has a direct influence on your payment. You can earn a bonus up to £1.80 if your store reaches the target. Setting higher targets increases the potential bonus payment you can receive. However, if your store does not achieve your target, there will be no bonus payment. Starting at £0.40, your bonus payment increases by £0.20 for every $1,000 of sales beyond $8,000. This is shown by the blue line on the graph below.

If you feel unsure about your decision or if you want additional information, you can always ask your supervisor for advice. With their expertise and experience, the supervisor can make a very accurate guess about future sales, providing you with another forecast of future sales which is very close to the actual sales. However, for the time and effort required to help you, your supervisor will reduce your bonus payment. Hence, your bonus will be reduced by £0.30 if you ask your supervisor for advice (gray line).

Please note that if you fail to reach your targets, your bonus payment will be £0.
